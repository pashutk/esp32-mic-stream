ESP32-Mic Installer

WiFi microphone streaming for M5Stack Atom Echo

Connect your Atom Echo via USB and click the button below:

Requirements

  • M5Stack Atom Echo
  • USB-C cable
  • Chrome or Edge browser

After Installation

  1. Connect to the "ESP32-Mic-Setup" WiFi network
  2. Configure your WiFi in the captive portal
  3. Stream audio at http://esp32-mic.local/stream.wav
